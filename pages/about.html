Our

Us Year-9 TLLT team members want the parks to be welcoming, inclusive places where every community member feels comfortable, valued, and connected. Our vision is to build a park system shaped by the people who use it; one that evolves over time based on the ideas, needs, and experiences of residents. By encouraging shared responsibility, we aim to create parks that truly feel like a collective effort, places that everyone can take happiness in and enjoy. Ultimately, we strive for a park environment that reflects the community’s voice and continue to improve through ongoing collaboration.

Our

We Year-9 TLLT students will develop an easy-to-use digital suggestion platform that gives all residents a simple, accessible way to share their ideas, concerns, and feedback about the parks. This tool will make it easier for community members to speak up and for park leaders to understand what matters most to them. By collecting diverse input and organizing it in a clear, actionable way, the platform will help park leaders make informed decisions that reflect real community priorities. Our mission is to ensure that every resident’s voice can influence how the parks grow and improve, making community feedback a central part of daily operations and long-term planning.

Our Goals of creating this website

  • To make sharing ideas more efficient and easy
  • To create a community based smart park
  • To let people share their ideas freely
